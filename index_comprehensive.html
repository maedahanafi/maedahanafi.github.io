About me

Hello! I am Maeda F. Hanafi. I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at New York University and doing research work at the Abu Dhabi campus. I am interested in human-in-loop systems, example-driven program synthesis, and data visualizations. My favorite programming language is Java.

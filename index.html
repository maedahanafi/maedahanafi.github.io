About me

Hello! I am Maeda F. Hanafi. I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at New York University. I work with the Design Technology Lab at New York University's Abu Dhabi campus under the supervision of Prof. Azza Abouzied. My work mainly revolves around human-in-loop systems, program synthesis, data extraction, and visualization.

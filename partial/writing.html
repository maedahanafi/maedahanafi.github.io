Journaling
These are my hard copy journals that I've had since 2007 up until I got my first laptop in 2011 when I began journal writing digitally. From 2007 to mid-2020, I wrote approximately a total of 2,340 pages, all single-spaced. This began as a weekly habit and eventually became almost a daily, half an hour long habit. I wrote a lot about my experience throughout college, including people I've met and their stories and perspectives. This helped me gain confidence writing research papers, technical pieces, and articles, as showcased in this page.

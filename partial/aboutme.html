About me!
Welcome!
I am currently a research scientist at IBM Research Almaden. I build human-in-the-loop solutions in the space of HCI and AI for Conversational AI systems and Natural Language Processing (NLP) tasks.
I received my PhD in Computer Science from New York University in 2020. I was also a recipient of the Global Ph.D. Student Fellowship that funded my studies and research. I received by B.S. in Computer Science from Southern Connecticut State University, where I was awarded the Academic Excellency Award in Computer Science.
During my graduate studies, I interned at IBM and Lockheed Martin where I developed and designed tools and solutions for various applications such as information extraction, debugging, and data annotation. In 2020, my research work helped Lockheed Martin cut down $4.2 million worth of costs towards human-based aircraft inspection. In 2023, I received a Research Accomplishment Award at IBM for my research work that enabled end-users to extract text by highlighting text examples.
